# 封面文生图 Prompt

Create a highly detailed technical cover infographic in hand-drawn sketch style on a pure white background. The theme is: "How to design a high-quality Skill". The audience is experienced programmers who have already written Skills or `SKILL.md` files and now want to make them trigger more accurately, run more steadily, and fail in ways they can actually debug.

This is the cover image of a 3:4 vertical infographic series. It should feel sharp, useful, and worth clicking, but still look like an internal engineering review board rather than a pop-tech poster.

The cover should not explain definitions. It should throw out the real problems people run into when they try to write Skills in practice.

**Layout Structure:**

**Top Section (Header Area - 18% height):**

- Main title in bold large Chinese font:
  "如何设计一个高质量的 Skill？"
- Subtitle below in smaller Chinese font:
  "别只盯功能描述，先把命中、边界、流程、维护写对"
- The top should feel like a technical postmortem headline
- Use only very subtle drafting marks in one corner
- Keep the header clean and forceful

**Middle Section (Core Diagnosis Area - 50% height):**

Create one large central diagnosis board.
It should feel like an engineering review panel, not a decorative hero illustration.

At the center, place a strong Chinese conclusion block:

- Main line:
  "高质量 Skill，不是写得更长"
- Second line:
  "而是把触发、执行、失败都写具体"

Around this center block, place 4 to 6 short Chinese problem labels as isolated cards.
Recommended visible labels:

- "为什么总是不触发"
- "为什么老是误触发"
- "为什么一换环境就变脆"
- "为什么 SKILL.md 越写越肿"
- "为什么 fallback 让问题更难查"
- "为什么写了一堆说明，质量还是没上去"

These labels should feel like real questions from people who have actually踩过坑.
Do not make them sound like textbook headings.

Important composition rule:

- do not connect every label with lines
- do not create a spider web or radial spoke diagram
- use spacing, grouping, contrast, and placement instead of many connectors
- if any connector is used, keep it to one or two short structural marks only

**Bottom Section (Series Preview Area - 32% height):**

Add a clear Chinese section title:
"接下来，看看这 4 个问题"

Under it, create 4 compact preview cards in a clean 2 x 2 arrangement:

- "先看问题出在哪"
  "一个普通 Skill，到底差在哪"
- "再看该怎么改"
  "名字、描述、边界、流程怎么改对"
- "然后补齐关键块"
  "完成定义、失败信号、权限约束怎么补"
- "最后看怎么验收"
  "改完之后，怎么知道它真的变好了"

At the bottom, place a final strong Chinese punchline:
"从‘能跑’到‘能用稳’，差的不是功能，是你怎么改稿"

**Visual Style:**

- Professional engineering hand-drawn sketch style
- Pure white background with restrained blue-gray drafting accents
- Architecture review aesthetics, not pop infographic aesthetics
- Dense, but clean and disciplined
- Cards, compact diagnosis blocks, subtle warnings, technical note feeling
- No decorative overload
- Strong visual hierarchy from title -> diagnosis core -> preview cards
- The whole image should feel serious, useful, and immediately scannable

**Typography:**

- All visible text inside the image should be Chinese as much as possible
- Only keep English or mixed-language terms when they are necessary technical terms:
  - Skill
  - SKILL.md
  - fallback
- Chinese title must be visually dominant
- Annotation text should be short and natural
- Avoid stiff wording that sounds like an autogenerated contents page

**Color Palette:**

- Main structural color: deep blue-gray
- Highlight accents: teal
- Warning accents: muted orange
- Risk accents: restrained red
- Background: pure white
- Avoid heavy gradients

**Composition Constraints:**

- Vertical 3:4 aspect ratio
- Optimized for social media cover readability
- Avoid crowded connector clutter
- Avoid too many connectors
- Use whitespace as an organizing tool
- Keep the image readable at a glance in 3 to 5 seconds
- Around 45% structured blocks/cards, 20% text, 35% whitespace

**Negative Guidance:**

- Do not generate a generic AI poster
- Do not use sci-fi neon glow, glossy 3D surfaces, cinematic lighting, or fantasy effects
- Do not use robots, mascots, or human characters
- Do not make it look like a beginner tutorial cover
- Do not overload the center with arrows
- Do not turn the cover into a complex flowchart

Style: white-background technical hand-drawn engineering cover infographic, architecture diagnosis board, Chinese-first typography, minimal connectors, high-signal layout for experienced programmers
